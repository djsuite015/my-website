About DJ Suite

DJ Suite specializes in hip hop mashups, underground remixes, and exclusive mixtape edits.

Based in the US, DJ Suite has been mixing since the 1990's mainly mixing house and freestyle music. He took a very long break focusing on another career, however the itch has returned and he is back now working on fire!

If you enjoy hearing new takes on popular rap songs then this is the page for you. Dj Suite covers all musical genre's so you never know what you may hear and experience. Take a listen, you will not be disappointed.
