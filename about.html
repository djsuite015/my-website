About DJ Suite

DJ Suite specializes in hip hop mashups, underground remixes, and exclusive mixtape edits. Based in the US, DJ Suite has been mixing since the 90's and took a long break focusing on another career, however he is back now. If you enjoy hearing new takes on popular songs then this is the page for you. Dj Suite covers all musical genre's so you never know what you may hear and experience.
